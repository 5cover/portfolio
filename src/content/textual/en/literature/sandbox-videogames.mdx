    <h4>Technocracy and the Great Reset</h4>
    <p>
      Okay. A few months after this rather frustrating experience I joined Technocracy. This sub-server is similar to
      Spacecross, except that it is not oriented towards space conquest but industrialization on Earth. Which is, in the
      end, what I wanted. And so a claim system is proposed to protect its constructions from looting.
    </p>
